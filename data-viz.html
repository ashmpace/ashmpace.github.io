Data Visualizations

Created with Python and plotly, these figures illustrate the risk of automation to various U.S. industries across states. A value of one means a risk equal with that of the national average, a value of two, twice the national average, etc. These 'location quotients' were calculated using probabilities from the McKinsey Global Institute and occupational data from the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics.

Click on the graphics for more detailed interactivity.
